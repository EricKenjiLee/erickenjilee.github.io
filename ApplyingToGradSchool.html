Applying to Graduate Schools in Neuroscience (Ph.D.)

Being back home on Oahu I've had quite a lot of time to think about my experiences applying to Ph.D. programs: what I did right, what I did wrong, and the things I wish I had known before applying. What comes next is a collection of my thoughts regarding this process which was at times unsparing I've tried to think deeply about what I could possibly add to the conversation given the already excellent contributions to the "Applying to Grad School" literature — I wasn't a better applicant than others, I hadn't ever been involved in admissions, and I made a ton of mistakes in the application process.
