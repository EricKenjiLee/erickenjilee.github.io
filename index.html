Blogging Philosophy

Here I'll include some thoughts on my research from time-to-time; for posts on my research, I'll commit to writing a version of each post for two audiences:

  • The general public presuming background at the "high-school" level with a focus on background and impact
  • Scientists of resident and neighboring sub-disciplines with a focus on details

This approach comes out of my belief that we as scientists have a dual obligation to the public who've entrusted us to do good work and to other scientists whom might benefit from new knowledge.
