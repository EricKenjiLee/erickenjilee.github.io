Welcome to Neurotenic, the data blog of Eric Kenji Lee

Here I'll include some thoughts on my research from time-to-time; for posts on my research, I'll commit to writing three versions for each post for three audiences:

  • The general public presuming background at the "high-school" level with a focus on background and impact
  • Non-quantitative scientists of neighboring subdisciplines with a focus on findings
  • Peers in my field at the level of a specialized journal article with a focus on methods

This approach comes out of my belief that we as scientists have a triple obligation to the public who've entrusted us to do good work, to other scientists whom might benefit from new knowledge, and to the peers in our field of whom we rely upon.
